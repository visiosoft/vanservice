Common Questions

Everything you need to know about renting a van with us. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

What types of vans do you offer for rental?

We offer premium vans including Toyota Hiace and Nissan NV350 models. Our fleet includes both standard and luxury options, perfect for various needs from family trips to business transport. All our vans are well-maintained and regularly serviced for your comfort and safety.

What are your rental rates?

Our rates start from AED 299/day for standard vans and AED 699/day for luxury vans. Long-term rental discounts are available. All rates include basic insurance and 24/7 support. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

What documents do I need to rent a van?

You need a valid driving license, passport/Emirates ID, and a credit card for security deposit. International visitors need their passport and international driving permit. All documents must be original and valid for the entire rental period.

Do you provide insurance coverage?

Yes, basic insurance is included in all rentals. Additional comprehensive coverage is available for extra peace of mind.

Is there a mileage limit?

Our standard rental includes 250 km per day. Additional kilometers can be purchased at competitive rates.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer free delivery and pickup within Dubai. Additional charges may apply for other emirates.
