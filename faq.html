Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our van rental services. If you have additional questions, feel free to contact us.

What types of vans do you offer for rental?
We offer a variety of vans, including cargo vans, passenger vans, and luxury vans. Each van is well-maintained and suitable for different needs. Learn more about our van options.
What are the rental rates?
Rental rates vary depending on the type of van, rental duration, and additional services. Please contact us for a detailed quote.
Do I need a special license to rent a van?
For most vans, a standard driver's license is sufficient. However, for larger vehicles, additional requirements may apply. Check with us for details.
Can I rent a van for one-way trips?
Yes, we offer one-way rentals. Additional fees may apply depending on the drop-off location. Visit our services page for more information.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before the rental period starts without any penalty. Late cancellations may incur a fee.
Do you offer insurance for rentals?
Yes, we offer optional insurance coverage for all rentals. You can choose from different plans based on your needs. Learn more on our services page.
